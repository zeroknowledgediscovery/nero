Estella chuckled, undeterred by Morgana’s whimsy. "But the joy of a delightful meal, Morgana, is a memory that endures far beyond the moment. The rivalry was sealed with as much swiftness as it was silent, a quiet undertow rippling beneath the lush revelry of castle life. Estella took the gesture with cautious gratitude, yet felt the vine of doubt creeping into her heart. The tapestry hung in her chambers, vibrant yet menacing, a monument to Morgana’s influence—a curiosity as much as it was a curse.
