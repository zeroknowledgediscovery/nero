Yet, this simple desire was not met with universal approval. Many of the townsfolk, sturdy traditionalists at heart, found themselves offering kindly warnings.

"Harold, you're setting yourself up for more than you can manage," Mr. Connors next door had remarked as they leaned on the fence that marked the boundary of their lands. "Dogs take more time and trouble than they're worth, especially when you're a man as busy as you are."

It was around this time that the winds of chance brought a change. Dr. Lisa Menken, a newly graduated veterinarian, moved into the cottage down the lane. The moment she set foot on her patch of earth, Eugene seemed to breathe a thankful sigh.
