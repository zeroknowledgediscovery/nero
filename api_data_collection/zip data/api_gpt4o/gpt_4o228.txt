Now, this was no modest aspiration, not for a king whose hands were expected to wield scepters and swords. Yet Alasdair found himself captivated, almost bewitched, by the thought of strings singing beneath his fingers. A sound—a soulful whisper—that seemed to echo around the quiet chambers of his heart. Yet such frivolities were frowned upon in regal circles.
