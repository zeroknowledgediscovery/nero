While a soul was being rebuilt through the quiet ritual of soap and water, a planet was screaming. It was her first act of complex communication, a confirmation of a shared past, even if that past was only ten minutes old. She placed one half back on her plate and, with a trembling but determined hand, held the other half out to him. It was an offering. A bridge.
