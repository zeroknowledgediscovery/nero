Warriors. Organized. Intelligent. Armed.

The irony wasn't lost on her that while she cultivated life, death itself had apparently learned to cultivate warfare. Lyra's fingers traced the condensation on the glass, leaving streaks that caught the pale sunlight filtering through the perpetual haze that had settled over the valley weeks ago.

Twenty-three acres of carefully orchestrated growth, a testament to her belief that nature, when properly guided, could solve humanity's greatest challenges.
