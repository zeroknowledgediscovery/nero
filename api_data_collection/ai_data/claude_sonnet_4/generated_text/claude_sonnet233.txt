Lyralei Moonwhisper measured no more than six inches in height, her translucent wings catching the filtered sunlight that dappled through the canopy above. The pixie community of Santa Rosa had long whispered about the peculiar child who had appeared among them nearly two decades ago. Elder Fernwick, the most venerable of their kind, claimed to remember her arrival during a particularly violent thunderstorm, when lightning split the sky and the veil between worlds grew thin. She had been found curled beneath a mushroom ring, no memory of her origins, speaking in fragments of languages that predated even the oldest oak trees.

"Lyralei, you're doing it again," chimed Petal, her closest friend, whose rose-colored hair always smelled faintly of wild strawberries. "That faraway look, like you're seeing something the rest of us can't."

They perched together on a branch overlooking the Pixie Council Glade, where the morning assembly was about to commence.
