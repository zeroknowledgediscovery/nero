Well, we’ll have to sleep with Minnie May--and you can’t think how she kicks.”  Miss Josephine Barry did not appear at the early breakfast the next morning. Mrs. Barry smiled kindly at the two little girls.  “Did you have a good time last night? I tried to stay awake until you came home, for I wanted to tell you Aunt Josephine had come and that you would have to go upstairs after all, but I was so tired I fell asleep. I hope you didn’t disturb your aunt, Diana.”  Diana preserved a discreet silence, but she and Anne exchanged furtive smiles of guilty amusement across the table.
