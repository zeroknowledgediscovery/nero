Therefore I have no brains, and I come to you praying that you will put brains in my head instead of straw, so that I may become as much a man as any other in your dominions.”  “Why should I do this for you?” asked the Lady.  “Because you are wise and powerful, and no one else can help me,” answered the Scarecrow.  “I never grant favors without some return,” said Oz; “but this much I will promise. If you will kill for me the Wicked Witch of the West, I will bestow upon you a great many brains, and such good brains that you will be the wisest man in all the Land of Oz.”  “I thought you asked Dorothy to kill the Witch,” said the Scarecrow, in surprise.  “So I did.
