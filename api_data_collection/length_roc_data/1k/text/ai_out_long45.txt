“I wondered how long you would take,” she said, her voice quiet, almost amused.

“I thought there were only seven,” Kaelen said.

“There were. Until you.”

Her gaze was steady, though sadness pooled at its edges. “Each shard you recovered was a fracture of the original source. Long ago, before memory was memory, there was only one truth.
