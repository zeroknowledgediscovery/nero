The game was over. The lie had failed. This," he gestured to the floating symbols, to the memory of the sonnet, to the entire invisible scaffolding of his resistance, "this was all for her. Lena felt the silence before she understood its cause. It began as a subtle shift in the campus network, a dead spot where there had once been a faint, complex signal.
