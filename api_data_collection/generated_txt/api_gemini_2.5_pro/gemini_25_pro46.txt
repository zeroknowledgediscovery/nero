In the days that followed, Amélie felt the change in the very air around her. When she walked to the market on Saturday morning, her journey was punctuated by cheerful greetings. She was no longer just Mademoiselle Dubois, the schoolteacher. She was Amélie, the woman who had brought the tango to town.
