Today, however, something was different. The humming carried an unfamiliar melody, one that seemed tinged with excitement rather than the usual contemplative quality he had grown to love. Patrice struggled to find his voice, aware that he was staring at her with an expression that probably revealed far too much of his feelings. "Of course, Miss Whitmore. I would be delighted to help you with whatever you need."

She led him to her apartment, which he was seeing for the first time despite months of curious speculation about its interior.
