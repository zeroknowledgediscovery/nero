The morning light filtered through the thin curtains of Paddy O'Sullivan's bedsit in Naas, casting long shadows across the cramped space he'd called home for the past fifteen years. At seventy-three, Paddy had grown accustomed to the ritual of checking his post with the same mixture of hope and resignation that had characterized most of his later years. Your unique vocal talents and heartfelt performance have impressed our judges, and we would like to invite you to perform in the grand finale at the London Palladium."
