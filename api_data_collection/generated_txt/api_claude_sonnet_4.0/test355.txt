Unlike other royalty who concerned themselves with conquest and crown jewels, Alessandro's passion lay in the delicate art of cuisine. His small principality, nestled between the Hudson River and the sprawling urban landscape, had become renowned not for its military might but for its extraordinary culinary traditions.

Alessandro forced a diplomatic smile. "Prince Damien, what brings you to Jersey City? Surely not a desire to sample our renowned cuisine?"

Damien laughed, a sound like breaking glass. "Hardly. I've come to discuss a proposition that will benefit us both. Your territory sits in a most advantageous position, and I have plans that require... expansion."

The threat hung in the air between them like smoke from a burnt dish. Alessandro had dealt with Damien's aggressive tendencies before, but something about this visit felt different, more ominous. Whatever Damien had planned, Alessandro would not allow it to destroy the community he had worked so hard to build.

The palace kitchen hummed with activity as Alessandro and his guest were seated in the intimate dining room overlooking the city. Through the large windows, the lights of Jersey City twinkled like stars, each one representing a life Alessandro felt responsible for protecting.
