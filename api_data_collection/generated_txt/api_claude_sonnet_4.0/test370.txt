The construction had been controversial – many locals opposed turning agricultural land into a playground for wealthy tourists – but the town council had been swayed by promises of jobs and increased revenue.

Giorgos had watched the transformation from his hillside vantage point, observing as bulldozers carved fairways from ancient terraces and as sprinkler systems replaced gnarled olive trees.
